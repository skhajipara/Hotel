A Taste of Tradition

* Our Uniqueness

~ In Food

  • Our restaurant uses directly farmed vegetables.
  • We use pure peanut oil, extracted directly from farmers' peanuts.
  • Pulses and agricultural items are supplied directly from farmers.
  • Dairy products like ghee and milk are provided by our own buffalo stable.

~ Serving Style
(Only Available in Village Vibe)

  • We use brass utensils to serve food, just like in the past.
  • We embrace traditional ways of serving to honor our heritage.
  • All signature dishes are prepared perfectly in clay pots.

~ Dining Arrangements

  • Traditional dining area with a seating capacity of 550 people.
  • Separate private dining areas for special occasions and events.
  • A dedicated, fun dining area for children.
  • Authentic floor seating in perfect Kathiawadi style.
  • Fully accessible separate dining areas for people with disabilities.
