Welcome to S.K. Restaurant

* Our Uniqueness

~ In Food

       - Our restaurant uses directly farmed vegetables. 
       - Our restaurant uses pure peanut oil, which is extracted directly from peanuts grown by farmers. 
       - Pulses and other items that depend on agriculture are supplied directly from farmers. 
       - Dairy products like ghee and milk are provided by our buffalo stable.

~ Serving Style

       - We are using brass utensils to serve food, just like in the past.
       - We are using a traditional way of serving food, just like in the past.
       - All dishes are prepared in clay pots.

~ Dining arrangements

       - We have a traditional dining area with a seating capacity of 50 people.
       - We have a separate dining area for special occasions and events.
       - We have a separate dining area for children.
       - And there is also an arrangement to eat in the perfect Kathiawadi style, just like in the village.
